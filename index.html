Paraná

Paraná is a state in the southern region of Brazil. It is bordered by São Paulo to the north, Mato Grosso do Sul to the northwest, Paraguay to the west, Argentina to the southwest, Santa Catarina to the south, and the Atlantic Ocean to the east. The state is a hub for agriculture, manufacturing, and energy production, with a significant focus on hydropower. Curitiba, the state capital and largest city, is renowned for its urban planning, environmental initiatives, and high quality of life. Paraná is also home to stunning natural landscapes, such as the Iguaçu Falls, a major tourist attraction.
